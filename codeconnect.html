CodeConnect

DESCRIPTION:

Created this high-fidelity prototype with the most loveliest team of three developers as a semester-long project during our undergraduate study. Inspired by the popular social platforms, Tinder and Reddit, this prototype simulates as a mobile application intended for college postgraduates wanting to develop their social network professionally or personally for platonic friendships. Users can swipe on profiles or search for those with similar interests, send follow requests, direct message, and share/gain career advice with those who have also graduated using the dynamic forum.

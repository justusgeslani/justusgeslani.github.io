Caffinder

DESCRIPTION:

Created this web app with an absolutely delightful team of three developers as a semester-long project during our undergraduate study. Inspired by the popular location service, Google Maps, this application is intended for college students wanting to find the nearest coffee shops or cafe restaurants for a well needed caffeinated fix or newfound/go-to study spot. Users can search beyond their local area or click on any available coffee shop/cafe nearby to view its address, opening/closing status, ratings, average price, and driving distance/time. Additionally, users have the option to set up a profile to add coffee shops/cafes to their favorites list.
